## Guidelines

- **Keep entries comprehensive but scannable** (5-10 minutes reading time)
- **Include concrete links** to code, KG nodes, issues, and artifacts
- **Track decisions and rationale** for future reference
- **Document both successes and learnings** from challenges
- **Update KG references** when decisions are made
- **Link to related Santiago code** implementations
